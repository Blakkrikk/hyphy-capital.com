Strategy
The goal is to understand the performance difference between a regular Retail Fund driven by current thematic topics (ESG, Crypto, Big Tech, etc.) versus a Quant Fund driven by automated quantative strategies through Machine Learning Algorithms. The goal is to understand and determine the best way of investing. Hence the starting hypothesis is that there will be no difference between the Retail Fund and the Quant Fund. The methodology is to track return and risk metrics through out the year 2021. This website will be used to publish results and conclusions of this research
