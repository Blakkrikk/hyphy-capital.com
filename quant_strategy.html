Fund Setup

Hyphy Capital's Quantitative Fund is a mathematical Fund based on Google's Open-Source Keras Deep Learning Library that aims to completely eliminate human decision making and is solely based on historic and live financial data. The fund's models are deployed via the Numerai Hedge Fund. Numerai is a San-Francisco based Hedge Fund using crowd-sourced trading signals. The crowd (Data Scientists or any particpant) is incentivized through a payout mechanism that rewards accurate signals (stock price predictions) and punishes inaccurate signals.

It does this by accepting Numeraire (NMR), their in-house ERC-20 token built on the Ethereum Blockchain. Particpants can use NMR to 'stake' on their own models. Numerai then pays out NMR to each model, depending on its accuracy. Hyphy Capital's Quant Fund is built on 5-6 models, which are used to submit signals on Numerai. Thus, the Fund's Current AUM is, in reality, denominated in NMR. Though the Fund keeps a liquid NMR cushion (5-10% of the total AUM) that is not 'staked' towards the deployed models. This is to remain adaptable to market conditions if necessary ('unstaking' NMR from Numerai may take about 28 days).

The staking/unstaking and payout mechanism happens decentralized via smart contracts on the Ethereum Blockchain and can all be verified either via the Etherscan or the Numerai Signals Leaderboard.
